Why Skiing Is the Best Winter Hobby

Skiing is my favorite way to get outside in the winter. It mixes exercise, challenge, and time with friends. Every run feels a little different depending on the snow, the light, and how I am feeling.

Things I Love on the Mountain

  1. The feeling of carving turns
    • Linking smooth S-turns down a groomer
    • Hearing the quiet scratch of edges on snow
    • Leaning into the turn and trusting the skis
  2. Being above the clouds
    • Riding the lift through fog into sunshine
    • Seeing whole valleys covered in white
  3. Time with friends
    • Lap after lap on our favorite runs
    • Laughing after the occasional wipeout
    • Hot chocolate in the lodge at the end of the day

One of my dream days: blue sky, fresh snow, and empty runs.

Basic Ski Gear Checklist

You do not need the most expensive equipment to have fun. You just need gear that fits and keeps you warm and safe.

Essential Gear

  • Skis and bindings
  • Ski boots that actually fit
  • Helmet and goggles
  • Warm waterproof gloves
  • Ski jacket and pants

Nice-to-Have Extras

  • Neck gaiter or balaclava
  • Hand warmers
  • Backpack for snacks and water
  • Action camera for your best runs
